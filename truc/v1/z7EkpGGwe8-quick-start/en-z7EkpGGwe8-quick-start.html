General

 

MiniMad is an all-in-one dedicated media playback device for video and LED mapping, based on the Raspberry Pi platform. Easy to use, small and versatile, it can be a good replacement for a computer in your future audiovisual installation.

The MiniMad works with a SD-card that contains both the system and the software of the MiniMad. The MiniMad system can be updated or reinstalled if needed, by following the procedure described in the chapter called “Flash your MiniMad SD-card”.

The MiniMad software is getting automatically updated when exporting from an updated version of Mad- Mapper.

MiniMad can either be installed with MiniMad Video to play video-mapping, or with MiniMad Light to play recorded DMX sequences.

Note that MiniMad Video and MiniMad Light cannot be installed at the same time on the same MiniMad.

Finally, the SD-card must be activated for each new MiniMad it is used into. The SD-card is already activated in any brand new MiniMad. If you want to use a SD-card other than the one the MiniMad shipped with, please make sure to flash the latest system by following the chapter called “Flash your MiniMad SD-card” prior to

activate it. At export time, MadMapper will remind you to activate when trying to export to a SD-card that

was not yet activated.
