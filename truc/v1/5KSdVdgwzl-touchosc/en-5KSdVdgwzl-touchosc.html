Touch OSC

The following TouchOSC layout controls both MiniMad Video and MiniMad Light.

 

 

  • Works exactly like the MiniMad physical buttons.

 

 

  • Touch the blue SYNC button on top-right at startup to receive the MiniMad status.

 

 

  • Hold “SHUTDOWN” for 2.5 seconds to turn off the MiniMad.

 

The layout is available in MadMapper Downloads.

The TouchOSC app is required to load and use the layout.
