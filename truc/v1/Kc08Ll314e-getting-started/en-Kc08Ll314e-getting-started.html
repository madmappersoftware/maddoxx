MadProjectorControl



The features needed by the pros

  • Compatible with any PJLink compliant device
  • Control any number of projectors
  • Cuable, schedulable and automatable
  • Allows power on/off and shutter remotely
  • Displays device information

Introduction


MadProjectorControl is a MadMapper paid extension using the PJLink protocol to remotely control any number of video projectors.

https://pjlink.jbmia.or.jp/english

 

Totally cueable, this extension allows to schedule and automate power and shutter activity. You can therefore remotely power your PJLink-enabled projectors on and off, as well as mute their video and audio signal at will. Then, with the help of MadMapper’s Calendar Scheduler & Cue Scheduler, you can fully automate your installation, removing the need for a dedicated operator.

 

Once purchased, MadProjectorControl can be installed on any MadMapper 5 (and above) system.

Please note that this guideline cannot cover an essential part: the network setup of your projector. While the use of MadMapper and the PJLink protocol remain the same, the projector setup varies from one model to another, from one brand to another. Be aware that this step can be complicated if you are not initiated!
